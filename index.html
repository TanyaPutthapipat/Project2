Project: The Creation Under Lord Theodore






The Creation Under Lord Theodore is a religion that believes in the Computer/ Simulation Theory, which was founded by philosopher Nick Bostrom. The computer theory is that you are in a computer generated world, which means you;re just lines of code. Your faith has already been decided for you and all your actions aren't your actions but some other being's. It's something like being in The Matrix, being in the game like the sims but also similar to the Truman show where everything you do is watched and monitors. Lots of T.V shows and movies present this idea, but have you ever stopped and think for a moment that this could be real.

My project is creating a religion. Plan out who do you worship, the leader of this community, how to practice The Creation Under Lord Theodore. I made this intending it would turn into a cult, which explains the name of my cult. Creation Under Lord Theodore. C U L T.
